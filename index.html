Bonsai

$19.99

The purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the viewer, and the pleasant of effort and ingenuity for the grower.

By contrast with other cultivation practices, bonsai is not intended for production of food or for medicine. Instead bonsai practice focuses on long-term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container.
